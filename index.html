Our Services

Employee Lifecycle Management

HR shared services engine that standardizes and centralizes the employee life cycle processes like On-boarding (including document management), transfer, confirmation, separation management etc.

On-boarding Management

We take care of all pre and post joining activities and deliver the best first 90 days experience to the new employees joining your organisation. Be it document management, Induction management, employee profile creation, and coordination for asset allocation, our team of experts manages each step to provide the new employee a memorable experience.

Employee Movements

Transfers and Promotions are amongst the most crucial employee lifecycle events. We help you in managing these transitions in a seamless manner.

Separation management

Separation or exit management in itself is a combination of complex sub processes like No dues clearance, full and final settlement etc. Our team helps you manage all these processes in a hassle-free manner, removing the pain of handling so many transactions

Employee Database Management

Centralized employee database management. A team of professionals trained on different HR systems help in keeping all your employee records up to date.

Employee Data Interface Management

One source employee data interface to payroll and allied services.

HR Dashboard and Analytics

Configurable HR Dashboard and analytics which provide meaningful insights to the clients for taking informed decisions.

HR Helpdesk

Robust HR Helpdesk to manage your employee and manager query. Providing multiple ways to interact with the employees and managers - web and email, our team of HR process experts provide the best possible solution to all employee queries.
